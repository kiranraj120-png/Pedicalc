Kiran Raj ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Pedi Calc mobile application (the "Service").

This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service and the choices you have associated with that data.

We value your privacy. Our goal is to provide a useful tool while collecting minimal or no personal information.

1. Information Collection and Use

We designed Pedi Calc to be a simple and private utility.

No Personally Identifiable Information (PII)

We do not require you to create an account, and we do not collect any personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, phone number, or physical location.

No Protected Health Information (PHI)

The calculations you perform within the app (such as patient age, weight, or drug dosages) are processed exclusively on your device. This data is not transmitted to us, stored on our servers, or shared with any third party. Once you close the app or clear the data, it is gone, unless you specifically save it locally on your own device (e.g., as a "favorite" or "recent calculation").

2. Analytics

We use third-party Service Providers to monitor and analyze the use of our Service.

Google Analytics for Firebase

Google Analytics for Firebase is an analytics service provided by Google Inc. We use this service to collect anonymous, non-personal data to help us understand how our Service is being used, identify which features are most popular, and to diagnose and fix crashes. This data may include information such as your device type, operating system version, and general usage patterns. It does not include any personal information or the specific calculations you perform. For more information on the privacy practices of Google, please visit the Google Privacy & Terms web page.

3. Advertising

Our Service is supported by ads, which are delivered by our third-party advertising partners.

These third parties may use cookies, device identifiers (like Google's Advertising ID or Apple's IDFA), or other technologies to collect information about your use of our Service and other apps and websites. This information is used to serve you with advertisements that are more relevant to your interests (interest-based advertising). We do not share any personal information or calculation data from the app with our advertising partners.

Google AdMob

AdMob is an advertising service provided by Google Inc. For more information on how Google uses data from its ad services, please visit: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads.

4. Data Security

The security of your data is important to us. Since all sensitive calculation data remains on your device, the primary security measure is the security of your device itself. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect any anonymous data we collect (as described in Section 2), remember that no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Children's Privacy

Pedi Calc is intended for use by medical professionals and/or adult caregivers.

This Service is not directed at anyone under the age of 13 ("Children"). We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your Child has provided us with Personal Data, please contact us. If we become aware that we have collected Personal Data from children without verification of parental consent, we will take steps to remove that information.

Our Service may contain links to other sites that are not operated by us (e.g., links to medical journals or drug reference websites). If you click on a third-party link, you will be directed to that third party's site. We strongly advise you to review the Privacy Policy of every site you visit.

We have no control over and assume no responsibility for the content, privacy policies, or practices of any third-party sites or services.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
